New Fast-Food Chain Makes Headway

Locals Shocked

Locals of Indiana, Pennsylvania were surprised to find out about a new fast-food restaurant, Chick-fil-A. This restaurant will be built in the spot of the once loved Rustic Lodge. A local high school student said, It’s crazy to see the small town I grew up in evolve so quickly in the last few years.

White Township officials approved the building, saying they think this will be an improvement for the town by creating new jobs, increasing traffic, and helping the economy.

Building is expected to start in April. Locals are starting to get excited. The once small town of Indiana isn’t so small anymore.
